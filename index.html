A Space Wizard, A Farm Boy, And A Scoundrel


Star Wars is a story that began in 1976. The creator, writer, and director of the first film, George Lucas, had high hopes to create a new mythology. More science fantasy than science fiction, the viewer is thrust into a world of space wizards, bounty hunters, alien gangsters, and an evil empire. Luke Skywalker (Mark Hamill) is a farm boy looking for adventure and he gets just that when a hologram of a beautiful woman (Carrie Fischer) appears before him asking to be rescued. With the help of former Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi (Alec Guiness), smuggler Han Solo (Harrison Ford), and furry friend Chewbaca, Luke must risk it all to save the princess from the clutches of the Dark Lord Vader.
